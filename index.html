My Latest Project: Trump's First 100 Days on Twitter

This project explored the tweets coming from the realDonaldTrump twitter account from Inauguration day to day 100. It shows the difference between the tweets that came from an android phone and iPhone. The Jupyter Notebook starts with interactive topic modeling of the android tweets, then the iPhone tweets, and finally the account over 100 days. The code for how I pulled in the Twitter data along with some exploratory data analysis is also available.
